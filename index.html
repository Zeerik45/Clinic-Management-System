Medical Service

Smith's Medical is Hospital’s Outreach Programme designed to conveniently provide quality healthcare services to patients closer to where they live and work, 24 hours a day 7 days a week. Our team of highly qualified, trained and experienced doctors, nurses, technicians and administrative staff is equipped to provide compassionate care, diagnosis and treatment for all our patients.

We offers 24/7 Urgent Care Services​ that are for patients who need medical attention that should be addressed on the same day. If the patient’s condition is life threatening, we can transfer the patient directly to our Main University Hospital Campus on Stadium Road for immediate care and treatment.
